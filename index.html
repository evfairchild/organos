Revolutionizing Drug Development with Tissue Chip Technology

Organos is a leader in the investigation of compound effects on cardiac tissue. Its robust platform combines a 3D human cardiac tissue models and an advanced in silico analysis platforms.

Advanced organ-on-chip technology to create beating micro tissues for testing

Integrated in silico modeling to determine drug mechanism of action and predict in vivo response

Microphysiological Systems

Integrated in silico Modeling

Organos 3D tissue model allows combined analysis of tissue electrophysiology, calcium handling dynamics, and cardiac contraction to provide insight into the key pathways of compound cardiotoxicity. Amenable for repeat testing and long term culture, these cost effective micorotissues provide physiologically relevant markers for evaluating compound safety.

Organos advanced in-silico system augments the experimental outputs of the system, mechanistically determining method of action from dose escalation studies, and prediction of effects in both relevant animal models and humans, including population effects and projection onto disease models.
